Here’s the English translation of each question 👇

1. 这个职位的典型一天是什么样？  
    → **What does a typical day in this role look like?**
2. 团队主要服务的客户是谁？  
    → **Who are the main clients or stakeholders that the team works with?**
3. 前三个月的主要期望是什么？  
    → **What are the main expectations for the first three months in this role?**
4. 团队成员最重要的品质是什么？  
    → **What are the most important qualities you look for in team members?**
5. 这个角色如何帮助实现部门目标？  
    → **How does this role contribute to achieving the department’s goals?**
6. 团队今年的重点目标是什么？  
    → **What are the team’s key priorities or goals for this year?**
7. 该职位的职业发展路径？  
    → **What does the career development path for this position look like?**
8. 如何衡量这个角色的成功？  
    → **How is success measured in this role?**

### **Questions to Ask**

Prepare some thoughtful questions in advance, such as:

- What does a typical day in this role look like?
- What are the key goals for the next 3 months or 1 year?
- Can you tell me about the team culture and opportunities for career development?
