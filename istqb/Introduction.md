# 0. Introduction
---

## Course Objective
 

This course aims to introduce the International Software Testing Qualification Board (ISTQB®) and its globally recognized certification scheme, focusing on the Foundation Level. The objectives of the course are:

  

1. **Introduce ISTQB® and Its Role**

Provide a clear understanding of the ISTQB® organization, its structure, and the value it brings to the software testing profession worldwide.

  

2. **Explain the Certification Path**

Clarify the ISTQB® certification structure, with an emphasis on the Foundation Level, including its scope, benefits, and how it fits into the broader career path of software testers.

  

3. **Support Learners Preparing for Certification**

Equip candidates with the core knowledge and skills required to pass the ISTQB® Foundation Level exam through structured content and practical examples.

  

4. **Promote International Standards in Testing**

Encourage the adoption of common language and practices in software testing by aligning course content with globally accepted ISTQB® standards.

  

5. **Contribute to Professional Development**

Help learners strengthen their testing foundation, support career growth, and improve the overall quality of software products and processes.

  

This course is intended for aspiring software testers, professionals seeking certification, training providers, and anyone interested in standardized testing practices.

---

  

## 0.2 The Certified Tester Foundation Level in Software Testing

  

The Foundation Level qualification is aimed at anyone involved in software testing. This includes:

  

- Testers

- Test analysts

- Test engineers

- Test consultants

- Test managers

- Software developers and development team members

  

Also suitable for:

  

- Project managers

- Quality managers

- Product owners

- Software development managers

- Business analysts

- IT directors

- Management consultants

  

Holders of the Foundation Certificate can pursue higher-level software testing qualifications.

  

---

  

## 0.3 Career Path for Testers

  

The ISTQB® scheme supports testing professionals at all stages, offering both breadth and depth:

  

- Core Advanced Levels: Test Analyst, Technical Test Analyst, Test Manager

- Expert Levels: Test Management, Improving the Test Process

- Agile Tracks: Agile Technical Tester, Agile Test Leadership at Scale

- Specialist Stream: e.g., Test Automation, AI Testing, Model-Based Testing, Mobile App Testing, Performance Testing, Security Testing, Domain-specific testing (e.g., Automotive, Gaming)

  

Visit [www.istqb.org](https://www.istqb.org) for updated certification pathways.

A Foundation Level Certified Tester can:

  

- **FL-BO1** Understand what testing is and why it is beneficial

- **FL-BO2** Understand fundamental concepts of software testing

- **FL-BO3** Identify test approaches and context-based activities

- **FL-BO4** Assess and improve documentation quality

- **FL-BO5** Improve testing effectiveness and efficiency

- **FL-BO6** Align testing with the development lifecycle

- **FL-BO7** Understand test management principles

- **FL-BO8** Write clear defect reports

- **FL-BO9** Understand testing priorities and effort factors

- **FL-BO10** Collaborate in cross-functional teams

- **FL-BO11** Understand test automation pros and cons

- **FL-BO12** Identify essential testing skills

- **FL-BO13** Understand risk's impact on testing

- **FL-BO14** Report on testing progress and quality
