# Disclaimer

This roadmap was created by the Platform Foundation team in December 2022.

This roadmap is a forward looking living document. It is always a work in progress and is subject to change without any prior notice. It will be reviewed quarterly at minimum. It is not a commitment to deliver any feature or functionality. 

It remains flexible and can adapt with the organisational and technology change of priorities and market (consumer) trends.

Each feature is always assessed on value to consumers and impact on achieving the Platform Foundation goals and delivered in thin slices of value to remain nimble. 

It is guided by the Platform Foundation vision.
